Case Labels are based on the week created.

The ISO-8601 date standard, gives a number for all weeks in a year, here it is converted to a letter and used to determine the relative creation date of the case at a glance. This page lists the current labels and labels for the year.
For further explanation on the ISO-8601 standard visit:
https://www.epochconverter.com/weeks

In this standard weeks start on Mondays. Some years have 53 weeks, that week is denoted using a hyphen(-). Since the letters restart after 26 weeks, labels in the second half of the year will repeat the first letter of the case name.
For example: ZBA... would be the first week in January, and ZZA... is the first week in July.
